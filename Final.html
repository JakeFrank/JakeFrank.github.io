Computer Security pertains to the regulation of accessibility to digital assets, whilst critically remaining focus on the key issues at hand. Namely, assets and regulation of access. Assets refer to the valuables, which need protection, ranging from company information to personal data, and ultimately privacy. Regulation of access demonstrates identification, authentication, and authorisation and therefore, who the user is, how the user can prove their identity, and the amount of access the user has to the information, which is attempting to be accessed. From old cryptographic systems to the computer security that exists today, privacy is the apex of an efficient and seamless computer security system.
